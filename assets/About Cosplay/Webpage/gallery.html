LoveLive!

LoveLive! is a School Idol Project. It revolves around a group of schoolgirls who wish to become idols. μ’s, one of the school idols, is main role of this anime. There are total of 9 members in μ’s and they become shining stars.

K-ON

K-ON is mainly about a group of schoolgirls who joined the light music club in their high school. They become best friends and have a fruitful life in their high school.

One Week Friends

One Week Friends talks about a schoolgirl who lose her memory about friends every week. Therefore, a boy try to befriend the girl again and again. As a result, the girl is no longer along and has more friends.

Kagerou Project

Kagerou Project is around a group of weird teenagers, who have supernatural suddenly. They investigate their odd phenomenon. At the same time, they are trapped in a time loop and there is a conspiracy behind.
